Product Usage and Care Manual

1. Laptops and Computers
Laptops should be kept in well-ventilated areas to prevent overheating. Use standard keyboards and external mice for ergonomic navigation during daily office work.

2. Audio Equipment
Headphones and Bluetooth speakers should not be exposed to direct water or extreme temperatures. Charge audio devices using the provided original USB cable only.

3. Beverage and Coffee Storage
Coffee beans and other dried food products must be stored in cool, dry places in airtight containers to preserve freshness and aroma.
