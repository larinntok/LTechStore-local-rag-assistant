Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is your warranty and return policy?
A: All technology products come with a 2-year official warranty. Returns are accepted within 14 days of purchase with the original receipt.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes. Orders over 5000 TL qualify for free express shipping.

Q: Which payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallet payments.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: Customer support is available 24/7 via email at support@ltechstore.com or through our official helpline.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5 business days after the returned product has been inspected.

Q: Can I exchange a product?
A: Yes. Products can be exchanged within 30 days if they are unused and in their original packaging.

Q: What are your shipping options?
A: We offer Standard Shipping (3–5 business days) and Express Shipping (1–2 business days).

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes. A tracking number is sent by email after your order has been shipped.
